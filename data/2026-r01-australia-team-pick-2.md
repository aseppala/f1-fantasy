## Alternative Swaps

### Swap 1: Hadjar ($15.1M) -> Hamilton ($22.5M)
- Ferrari driver + constructor synergy (double scoring)

- Drop Ocon ($7.3M) to Bortoleto (~$6.5M) or Lawson ($6.5M)

### Swap 2: Bottas ($5.9M) -> Lawson ($6.5M)
- Racing Bulls synergy with Lindblad
- Only $0.6M more, Lawson more likely to actually score
- Recommended if budget allows after verifying prices

- Costs ~$0.7M more
