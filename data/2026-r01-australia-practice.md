## Key Observations

### Top Teams
- **Ferrari:** Strongest in FP1 (1-2), both drivers consistently top 5 across sessions
- **McLaren:** Big step forward in FP2, Piastri fastest overall. Norris recovered from gearbox woes
- **Mercedes:** Both drivers in top 3 in FP2. Antonelli close to Piastri. Consistent with testing form
- **Red Bull:** Verstappen solid (P3/P6) but gap to frontrunners appears real. Hadjar P4/P9

### Midfield Standouts
- **Arvid Lindblad (Racing Bulls):** P5 FP1, P8 FP2 as a ROOKIE. Exceptional debut performance
- **Esteban Ocon (Haas):** P10 in FP2, shows Haas midfield competitive
- **Audi:** Both Bortoleto (P9 FP1) and Hulkenberg (P10 FP1) in top 10

### Teams in Trouble
- **Aston Martin:** Power unit disasters continue from testing. Alonso no timed lap in either session effectively. Stroll barely ran.
- **Cadillac:** Perez last with only 2 laps in FP2. Struggling new team.

### Pace Gap Analysis
- FP1: Ferrari 0.5s clear of Red Bull, then 0.5s to midfield
- FP2: Top 6 (Piastri through Verstappen) covered by ~0.5s
- Lindblad within 1s of top in FP1, top 8 in FP2 = exceptional for a rookie on a budget team
- Clear 3-team fight at the front: Ferrari, McLaren, Mercedes
