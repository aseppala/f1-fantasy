## Why These Picks

### Premium Picks: Leclerc + Antonelli

**Why Leclerc over Verstappen ($27.7M)?**
- Save $4.9M while getting a driver on a faster car
- Ferrari looked fastest or joint-fastest in ALL data sources
- Verstappen's Red Bull has tire degradation concerns (+7s to Piastri over race sim)

**Why Leclerc over Piastri?**
- Ferrari constructor doubles the value (Leclerc + Hamilton both scoring)
- Leclerc topped FP1, Piastri topped FP2 - roughly equal, but Ferrari constructor synergy wins

**Why Antonelli over Russell ($27.4M)?**
- $4.2M savings funds the budget picks
- P2 in FP2, matched Russell's pace
- Mercedes had BEST race pace in testing - both drivers benefit
- Differential pick: friends will default to Russell

### Value Picks: Bearman + Lindblad + Bottas

**Combined cost: ~$18-19M for 3 drivers**
- Bearman: proven fantasy value, overtaking points, only needs 9 pts for max price gain
- Lindblad: P5 & P8 in first-ever F1 practice sessions - at $5-6M this is a steal
- Bottas: $5.9M budget enabler, frees budget for 2 premium drivers + Ferrari constructor

### Constructors: Ferrari + Haas

**Ferrari:** Both drivers consistently top 5. Maximum constructor scoring.
**Haas over Racing Bulls:** Haas has proven F1 Fantasy value record. Two experienced drivers (Ocon + Bearman). More reliable scoring floor than Racing Bulls.
