### FP1 Key Observations

- **Mercedes dominant again** — Russell P1, Antonelli P2 just 0.12s behind. Continuation of Australia form.
- **McLaren clear 2nd team** — Norris P3, Piastri P4. Gap to Mercedes (~0.5s) smaller than to Ferrari (~0.3s behind McLaren).

- **Red Bull struggling** — Verstappen P8 (+1.8s), Hadjar P13 (+2.115s). Significant drop from top pace.
- **Bearman impressive again** — P7, best of the midfield, ahead of Verstappen.
- **Lindblad rough session** — P21 (+5.155s). Major drop from his strong Australia weekend (P5 FP1, P8 race).
