This is the hardest trio in the 2026 range — "The Hardest Trio" per CoffeeCorner preview.

## Graining & Degradation Notes

- **C3 Soft**: Highest graining risk, especially given extensive resurfacing of Sectors 2 and 3. New asphalt = low rubber laid in, first few stints unpredictable.
- **C2 Medium**: Expected primary race compound. Cool conditions help longevity.
- **C1 Hard**: Season debut. In cool temperatures, hard stints are manageable — 2025 saw most frontrunners start on Medium, pit ~lap 20 for Hard and run to the end.
- **Track evolution**: Resurfacing means 2025 data is a partial guide only — grip will build through practice/qualifying.
