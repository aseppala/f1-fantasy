# Circuit DNA Reference — 2026 Season

Season-level reference for all 24 circuits. Used by `/analyze-form` (circuit history), `/analyze-value` (DOTD probability, SC signal), and `/simulate` (overtaking index scaling).

**Overtaking Index:** 1 = Monaco (processional), 10 = Monza (DRS highway). Neutral = 5.
**SC Probability:** low (<20% historical SC rate), medium (20–40%), high (>40%).
**DOTD Profile:** Notes on what drives DOTD voting at each venue.
